## Quick SEO Wins

1. **Add Alt Text**: Ensure all images have descriptive alt text with keywords
2. **Internal Linking**: Link between related pages (e.g., About → Ventures → Freedom Aviation)
3. **Content Freshness**: Update dates and stats regularly
4. **Social Sharing**: Encourage shares with optimized OG images
5. **Local Citations**: Get listed on Colorado aviation directories
6. **Backlinks**: Reach out to Colorado aviation blogs/sites
7. **Google My Business**: Create listing for Freedom Aviation
8. **Testimonials**: Add customer reviews with schema markup
9. **Blog**: Start a blog about Colorado flying experiences
10. **Video**: Add flying videos with VideoObject schema

## Common Issues to Avoid

❌ Keyword stuffing (use keywords naturally)
❌ Duplicate content across pages
❌ Missing alt text on images
❌ Broken internal/external links
❌ Slow page load times
❌ Missing structured data
❌ Thin or duplicate meta descriptions
❌ Not updating sitemap when adding pages
❌ Ignoring mobile optimization
❌ No local SEO for Colorado
